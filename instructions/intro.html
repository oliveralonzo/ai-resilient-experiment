Welcome!

In this activity, you will review different visual versions of the same paragraph that have been edited to support skimming. The paragraphs will have some visual changes to emphasize which parts to read.

Throughout the activity, you will see two types of visual changes:

  1. Binary: In the second part of the activity, we will show you "binary" options. Binary means that text will either be emphasized or not. To skim, you can read only the emphasized parts to get the main content.
  2. Gradient: In the second part of the activity, we will show you "gradient" options. Gradient means that there will be different levels of emphasis. So, the changes will vary and you can choose to read the most emphasized parts.
